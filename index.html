Dashboard Overview

The project involves building an interactive dashboard to visualize the bacterial species living in a human naval regarding research for meat protein synthesis from human bacteria. The dataset catalogs the microbes that are colonizing human navels. The dashboard allows users to filter by individual subjects by showing demographic information and bacterial cultures within their naval.
